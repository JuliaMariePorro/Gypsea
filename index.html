STUCK, FULL OF EMPTY. 7-11-2024

I feel like I'm just surviving every day, hoping it ends, so it feels like I start living life again. When you miss home, where do you go? Is there any place I won't feel alone? I want to shift from this world, I want to matter to me. Stuck, lost, waiting to see, When this will be. My future has liability. It really feels like too much. Weighing me down, it's all so corrupt. Will the hurt end, am I capable of escaping this trend? I never want to mirror this again. Full of emptiness, I feel the absence so deeply, My heart is POURING out its PLEADING. For something so unsure, What is life's meaning?? Hard to decipher my needing. It's rain and thunder alike. Trying not to worry tonight. I'll take the next flight, When this storm ends, This version I'll never want to see again.

PERGATORY OF A LOST SOUL. 7-27-24

I am weakened, I am vulnerable My darkest parts are now audible. I used to not understand, why people weren't happy on land. I heard death's gossip all around me. I didn't get it, that thought never crossed me. Today I do. Haunted by giving in, saying goodbye to the lovely blue. I've joined the lost souls in their suffering. The blind trust in "it'll pass" needs to mean something.

WHO'S TURN IS IT? 7-28-24

"If they wanted to they would." A tale that needs to be understood? How can you tell when it's YOU who should? I take the chance on every fight, My heart like a knight. Pierced by love, never-ending bleeding. Put an end to this everlasting seeking. Amidst living in so much strife, The quest begs... Is it worth the fight? Leave it be and let it go? What if? WHAT IF? Give in first, let it all show. Is it me or you now, whose turn is it to go? The endless quest, To find this big heart at peace, at rest.

WADING, WAITING. 7-29-2024

The ocean is a desert, and there ain't much in it. You came in, the horizon turned velvet, A new sky lit. The stillness came to life. We've created a new purpose to life. I can feel now every ripple, the ocean's tiny movements. I like you to wade in my presence. It is cruel, though, to be aware of the seconds. You'll foresee the deadend, I noticed the drift, before you moved away. I could have prepared myself for this day. My persiting hope in the push and pull did'nt want to end. We'd always be attached to our invisible thread. Reaching for the string to pull you in when you float too far. Without it, we'll forever be apart. What if she's caught a glance at a different star. She'd have to cut it to travel those parts. We've swam out too far. And she'll figure out how to ride to shore faster than me. I'll be left out at sea, just me. A clear sky, and your'e gone. Now I wait for the clouds to come in, so I can see you again, beautifully drawn The day becomes a dream, to cope for when you leave. With one glance she reads that wave, my green eyed babe. Time to day dream from dusk till dawn, until I have strength to keep wading along.

FLAWED? 7-31-24

My writing focused on loss and despair, my mind all-consumed, obsessed, too emotionally aware. Its my perverse form of gratitude, for moments I once had. People that could make me sad, loves that were grand, flying high, never to land, experiences you could never think to plan, addictions in passion, I want to keep and have. The balance between living for now and after, I don't know if I can ever master. Building a life is too focused on my wants, Too much time to think on what I don't have, A painful glimpse of how the wants I once had slipped away. How can tomorrow be better, if it's gone today? I can't bear the planning, waiting, the effort, I always get hurt. By good people too. Id rather vehemently be happy right now, than live for a better tomorrow. Which is my biggest flaw.
